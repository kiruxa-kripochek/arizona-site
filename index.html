Arizona RP is the project that started the history of SAMP. The first server was opened on August 23, 2014.
Due to the rapidly growing popularity, 25 more servers were subsequently launched.
Therefore, by 2017, Arizona Role Play took the leading position in terms of attendance among all well-known Samp projects,
which it remains to this day.
